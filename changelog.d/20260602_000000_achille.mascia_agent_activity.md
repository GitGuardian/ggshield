### Changed

- `ggshield ai discover --history` now also collects raw AI-agent activity
  (transcript lines / database rows) from Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, Copilot
  CLI and VSCode and ships it to GitGuardian, which scans the content and strips
  secrets server-side. Large base64 blobs are elided before sending and the
  reporting machine/user is attached so the activity can be attributed.
